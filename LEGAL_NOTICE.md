# Legal Notice - Dark Web Access

## ⚠️ IMPORTANT LEGAL WARNING

**Dark web access via Tor has significant legal and ethical risks.**

### Legal Risks

1. **Accessing Illegal Marketplaces**
   - Accessing illegal marketplaces may be illegal in your jurisdiction
   - Even for research purposes, accessing illegal sites can have legal consequences
   - No clear legal protection for automated scraping of dark web sites

2. **Viewing Illegal Content**
   - You may inadvertently access illegal content (NCII, CSAM, etc.)
   - Viewing illegal content, even for research, can have legal consequences
   - Storing or transmitting illegal content is illegal

3. **Terms of Service**
   - Most dark web sites have no clear terms of service
   - Automated scraping may violate implicit terms
   - No legal protection if you violate terms

4. **Liability**
   - You may be liable for accessing illegal content
   - You may be liable for indexing illegal marketplaces
   - You may be liable if your tool enables illegal activity

### Ethical Considerations

1. **Enabling Illegal Activity**
   - Indexing illegal marketplaces may enable illegal activity
   - Providing intelligence about illegal sites may help bad actors
   - Consider the ethical implications of your work

2. **Privacy**
   - Dark web users may have legitimate privacy needs
   - Consider privacy implications of monitoring

3. **Content Filtering**
   - Must implement strict content filtering
   - Must never store illegal content
   - Must have procedures for handling illegal content

## Recommendations

### Before Using Dark Web Access

1. **Consult Legal Counsel**
   - Get written legal opinion about dark web access in your jurisdiction
   - Understand liability and legal risks
   - Document legal compliance procedures

2. **Implement Content Filtering**
   - Automated filtering for illegal content
   - ML models for content classification
   - Human review of findings
   - Never store illegal content

3. **Infrastructure Requirements**
   - Use dedicated servers (not public cloud)
   - Implement audit logging
   - Document all access
   - Have incident response procedures

4. **Operational Procedures**
   - Human review of all findings
   - Regular legal review
   - Compliance documentation
   - Insurance (if available)
